<en=2>New York 's main contract , light sweet crude for delivery in November rose 60 cents to close at 66.80 dollars per barrel .
<en=3>In London , the price of Brent North Sea crude for November delivery advanced 53 cents to 64.73 dollars .

<en=5>The US National Hurricane Center said Rita was " extremely dangerous " and could become a top - level category five storm on the Saffir - Simpson scale .
<en=6>The storm threatened oil installations in the Gulf of Mexico where about one - quarter of US oil operations are based .
<en=7>Oil companies evacuated offshore facilities as the storm 's progress kept global markets on tenterhooks .
<en=8>In Texas , BP shut its 460,000 barrels per day refinery and Marathon shut its 72,000 bpd plant .

<en=10>Exxon Mobil released nonessential staff from two giant Texas plants .
