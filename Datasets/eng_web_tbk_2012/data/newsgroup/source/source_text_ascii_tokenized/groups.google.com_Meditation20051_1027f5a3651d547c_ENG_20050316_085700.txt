<en=9>Kaoshikii is a dance which was invented in 1978 by Shrii Shrii A'nandamu'rti .
<en=10>It is a psycho-spiritual exercise and benefiting the mind by developing stamina and strength .
<en=11>Kaoshikii is especially good for women .
<en=12>It can ward off and cure a number of diseases , keeps the body youthful and enables an easy delivery in childbirth .
<en=13>Kaoshikii comes from the Sanskrit word ' kosa ' meaning shell or layer of mind .
<en=14>The self of the individual lies hidden in the innermost layers of existence ( kosas ) .
<en=15>Thus kaoshikii is a blossoming of the microcosm ( unit being ) in its attempt to attain a link with the Macrocosm ( Cosmic Consciousness ) .
<en=16>This attempt is normally referred to as mysticism .

<en=42>The Dance
<en=43>The eighteen steps of the dance are done rhythmically .
<en=44>First the dancer begins in the starting position : the hands together and raised over the head .
<en=45>Then to the beat of " dhin , dhin , ... , ta' , ta' " , the dancers begin their step , placing the big toe behind the heel of the other foot .
<en=46>In the final two steps , the dancers firmly stamp their feet on the ground .
<en=47>Kaoshikii can be danced for as many rounds as you like .
<en=48>--
<en=49>The Ideation
<en=50>When the dancers start the step with the beat of " dhin , dhin ... " , the mental ideation should also be kept .
<en=51>Here is the ideation .
<en=52>The two hands when upraised and folded together represent : " Now I am trying to establish a link with Parama Purus'a ( Supreme Consciousness ) . "
<en=53>Bending to the right indicates : " I know the right way to request You . "
<en=54>Bending to the left indicates : " I know how to fulfil Your demands . "
<en=55>Bending forward suggests : " I completely surrender to You . "
<en=56>Bending backward represents : " I am ready to face all obstacles that may come . "
<en=57>The last two steps represent : " O Lord I repeat Your cosmic rhythm . "
